Concept
- Tank battle is an open-world head-to-head tank combat game.
- Terrain will be used for tactical advantage.
- Focus on flow and feel.

Rules
- Move anywhere in the terrain, surrounded by mountains.
- Players start with finite health and ammo.
- Each direct hit takes away health.
- Last player standing wins.

Requirements
- SFX: Gun firing, explosion, barrel moving, turret moving,
	engine sound.
	
- Static mesh: simple tank comprising tracks, body, turret
			and barrel.

- Textures: Add for visual flare.
- Music: Background music to create tension.



Iterative Cycle
World -> Tank -> Controls -> Player 2 (AI) -> UI -> World
